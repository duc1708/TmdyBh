In everyday practice, T2DM patients often present with complex profiles that differ from populations studied in RCTs.1 Thus, RWE studies are essential to complement RCT data.2
Across multiple international RWE studies, Vildagliptin (Galvus®) and Vildagliptin + Metformin (Galvus®Met) consistently demonstrated good treatment response without tolerability issues, confirming their value as part of routine care in clinical practices around the world.2-5
